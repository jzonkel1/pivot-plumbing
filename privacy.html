Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Pivot Plumbing LLC ("Pivot Plumbing," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit pivotplumbing.biz or contact us for service. By using our website or submitting a request, you agree to this policy.

Who we are

Pivot Plumbing LLC is a licensed master plumbing company based in Flour Bluff, Texas, serving Corpus Christi and the Coastal Bend. You can reach us at (361) 460-2929 or fulcrum@pivotplumbing.biz.

Information we collect

Information you give us

  • Your name, phone number, and email address
  • Your service address or job location and details about the work you need
  • Any message or information you include in a form, call, text, or email

Information collected automatically

When you visit the site, basic technical data — such as your device type, browser, and the pages you view — may be collected through standard web logs and cookies used by our hosting and embedded services (see "Cookies and third-party services" below).

How we use your information

  • Respond to your request and provide a quote or estimate
  • Schedule, perform, and follow up on plumbing services
  • Contact you by phone, text message, or email about your request or service
  • Keep business records, process payments, and operate our business
  • Improve our website and services, and comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information.

Text messages & calls (SMS)

When you provide your phone number through our website, by text, or over the phone, you agree that Pivot Plumbing may call and text you about your service request, quote, scheduling, and related updates. Message and data rates may apply, and message frequency varies. You can opt out of text messages at any time by replying STOP, or reply HELP for help. Opting out of texts will not affect your ability to receive service by phone or email. Consent to receive text messages is not a condition of purchasing any service.

We do not sell or share your mobile phone number or your SMS opt-in information with third parties or affiliates for their marketing purposes.

Cookies & third-party services

Our website is hosted on Netlify and uses a small number of third-party services to function, including Google Fonts (typography) and Google Maps (our service-area map). These services may set cookies or collect limited technical data as described in their own privacy policies. Website forms are processed through Netlify Forms. We do not use your information for cross-site advertising.

How we share information

We share information only as needed to run our business and serve you: with service providers that host our website and process form submissions, with tools we use for scheduling or payment, and when required by law or to protect our rights. We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share your SMS opt-in information with third parties for marketing.

Data retention & security

We keep your information only as long as needed to provide service, meet our business and legal obligations, and resolve disputes. We use reasonable measures to protect your information, though no method of transmission or storage over the internet is completely secure.

Your choices & rights

  • Opt out of text messages any time by replying STOP; opt out of emails using the unsubscribe option or by contacting us.
  • Request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by emailing fulcrum@pivotplumbing.biz.
  • Depending on where you live (including Texas and California residents), you may have additional rights under applicable privacy laws. We will honor valid requests as required by law.

Children's privacy

Our website and services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Links to other sites

Our website may link to third-party sites (such as Google, the Better Business Bureau, or Nextdoor). We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of those sites.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes take effect when posted here, and we will update the "Last updated" date above.

Contact us

Questions about this policy or your information? Call (361) 460-2929 or email fulcrum@pivotplumbing.biz.
