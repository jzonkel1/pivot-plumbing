What we do

Full-service plumbing,
residential & commercial.

From a dripping faucet to a full repipe, Pivot handles it with the same standard: diagnose it properly, fix it cleanly, and explain it in plain English.

01

Leak Detection & Repair

Pinpoint slab leaks, hidden drips and pressure problems before they become water damage — then fix the source, not just the symptom.

02

Water Heaters

Repair, replacement and tankless upgrades. Faster hot water, lower bills, and installs that pass inspection the first time.

03

Drain & Sewer Cleaning

Stubborn clogs, slow drains and main-line backups cleared with camera inspection so you know exactly what's going on underground.

04

Repipes & New Lines

Whole-home repipes, water and gas line installs done to code — built to last in our coastal soil and water conditions.

05

Fixtures & Remodels

Faucets, toilets, sinks, tubs and full bath/kitchen rough-ins. Clean workmanship that looks as good as it works.

06

Commercial Plumbing

Restaurants, retail and property managers — dependable service, scheduled maintenance and fast response to keep your doors open.
