Good to know

Quick answers.

Do you charge for estimates?

For most standard jobs we'll give you an upfront, honest price before any work starts — no mystery fees and no pressure. Call and we'll talk through exactly what you need.

Do you take after-hours calls?

Pivot isn't a 24/7 emergency service, but if something goes wrong after hours, give Chad a call — if he's available, he'll come out. After-hours rates apply: $201 for the first hour, then $100.50 per additional 30 minutes.

What areas do you cover?

We're based in Flour Bluff and serve Corpus Christi, Portland, South Padre Island, Padre & Mustang Island, and the surrounding Coastal Bend. Not sure if you're in range? Just ask.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes — Pivot is owned and operated by licensed Master Plumber Chad Icke (RMP-43866) and carries $2M in general liability insurance. Every job is done to code.

Do you handle both residential and commercial plumbing?

Yes. We take care of homes across the Coastal Bend, and restaurants, retail spaces and property managers count on Pivot for dependable commercial service — fast response, scheduled maintenance, and repairs that pass inspection.

Who actually shows up to my house?

Chad Icke — the owner and a licensed master plumber. When you call Pivot you're talking to the person who does the work, not a call center or a sales tech.

Can you replace my water heater the same day?

Often, yes — depending on the schedule and the unit you need. We repair and replace tank and tankless water heaters, gas or electric, sized right and installed to code — and we can usually get your hot water back the same day.

Do you install tankless water heaters?

Yes — we handle tankless upgrades as well as standard tank replacements, for both gas and electric. We'll size it right for your home and install it to code so it passes inspection the first time.

How do you find what's actually wrong with my drain?

With a camera inspection. Slow drains, clogs and main-line backups get cleared fast — and we run a camera so you can see exactly what's happening underground before spending a dime on digging. We'll tell you straight whether it's a one-time clog or something bigger.
